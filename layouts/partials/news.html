GBCC is the flagship annual community conference for the Galaxy and Bioconductor projects. It brings together scientists, software developers, and trainers from a variety of disciplines to share their experiences, knowledge, and skills.

The conference will feature keynotes, invited talks, posters, trainings, and Birds of a Feather sessions. The conference will be held in a beautiful setting at the Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory (CSHL) in New York.
